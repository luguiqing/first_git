Call Shine. Broadening the experience of your phone calls!

Your calls even more interactive!

Create a previous text message and every time you call someone or someone calls you, your message will be automatically shown in the Display of the other person. You will be communicating even before the call is answered!

>> Know More

 

Why this service is useful

You can send a text to someone you are calling.

Imagine that you have something important to talk to someone, but you are not registered as a known contact in the person’s agenda. With Call Shine, you can customize a text and, when you make a call, the text will appear in the receiver’s Display. That way you can send information such as your name and why you want to talk to the person, increasing your chances to be answered.

You can send a text to someone that’s calling you.

Imagine that you are in an important meeting and you can’t receive an incoming call, but you want to inform the person calling you that you are busy and will call back later. With Call shine you can customize a text and, when someone tries to call you, it will appear in the person’s Display the text created by you informing that you are busy.

Customize your texts.

You can customize your texts for specific Contacts, Groups and Everyone. You also can set up the time and day of the week that the text will be sent. Also, you can set up a list of contacts to whom you don’t want to send any message.

The possibilites are huge.
Identification: Feel safe answering a call from an unknown number by receiving a Call Shine message with the person’s ID
Filter: During a meeting, choose to answer your mother for something urgent and choose call later a friend that calls about the soccer match on Sunday
Without signal: During trips with no network signal or low battery situations, with Call Shine you can let everyone know that you are OK and you will call later.
Safety: In a bus stop or other place you don’t feel comfortable picking up your cell and don’t want to act rude by not answering, set Call Shine message letting people know you are not available and call later
Good wishes: Wish Merry Christmas to everyone that you call or call you
Give a Justification: Go to a party or music festival and let your parents know you are OK even if you don’t answer the call
Differentiation: If you are a hiring company, let job candidates know you want to talk about a job opportunity
Professional situations: Use Call Shine as an “ID Card” and make an first professional approach to a prospective client
Practicality: Customize a romantic message for your partner
