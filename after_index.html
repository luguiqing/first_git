You five possibilities to choose
how send the messages

You can have three possibilities to choose who will receive the messages:

Everybody: the message will be send to everybody EXCEPT to people that have a message with Specific Contact or Group settings.

Specific contact: the message will be send to a specific contact.

Group: the message will be send to a group you created.

You have two ways to choose when you send the messages:

Time segment: you can choose a period of hours to send the message.

ending time: choose the day of week that you want send the message.

* If you create a message with the exactly the same settings you will receive a warning message to choose which message you want keep activated.
